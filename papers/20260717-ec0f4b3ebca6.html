PAPER INFORMATION

Spatiotemporal dynamics of scattering exceptional points

作者
Yu Xiao, Zhiyuan Che, Lei Shi, Xu Wang, Jie Zhu
期刊
Nature Communications
发表日期
2026-07-07
打开出版社页面

英文摘要

Exceptional points (EPs) offer new routes to control wave-matter interactions in non-Hermitian systems. Whereas conventional EPs arise in Hamiltonians and mark eigenstate coalescence, scattering EPs recast them in input-output responses for free-space wave control. Yet the rich topology of EPs is largely concealed in steady-state observables, exhibiting similar asymmetric patterns governed by identical Jordan-block forms. Here we exploit scattering as an evolving process in space and time to resolve spatiotemporal dynamics. We establish a cross-domain mapping from momentum-frequency to space-time, connecting static EPs to dynamic scattering singularities. By projecting a unitary scattering matrix onto lower-dimensional subspaces, EP topology is imprinted into a wave packet as a spatiotemporal vortex (STV), whose phase singularity is anchored to the EP and carries its quantized topological charge. In a liquid-surface-wave platform, we observe a charge-1 STV and a disintegrated charge-2 STV, generated by distinct EPs in a single device. Our work links fixed exceptional topology to dynamic wave topology, establishing a framework for spatiotemporal wave control. Exceptional points govern unique wave scattering, yet their topology is often hidden in steady states. Here, the authors map these static singularities to dynamic wave packets, revealing how their topological charge is imprinted onto spatiotemporal vortices using liquid surface waves.
